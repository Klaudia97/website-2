35°N

35°N is an exceptional product created by sun and fresh mountain air in the sunniest place of Europe

The Bottle

Limited First Edition

35°N Extra Virgin Olive Oil comes in 500 ml (16.9 fl oz) Limited First Edition glass bottle.

The bottle is coated in white matt color which protects the olive oil from direct sunlight and reduces surface temperature so you can enjoy the freshness and quality of the contents for longer.

It is gorgeous to look at and pleasant to hold due to its pebble like surface texture.

Cork lid with included pourer provides a convenient way to effortlessly pour the product over your food.

The bottle is 100% recyclable.

The Process

First Cold Press

Our olives are handpicked and transferred to a nearby mill where the 35°N Extra Virgin Olive Oil is extracted using a cold-press method to preserve its quality and taste.

The extracted oil then stays in oxygen-free containers for a while in order for it to settle naturally before bottling which reduces the need for filtering and increases the amount of preserved polyphenols.

Each bottle is then filled with olive oil and passes a multi-step manual quality control process before being packaged for final delivery.
